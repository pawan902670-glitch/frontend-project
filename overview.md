## 💡 Why this structure is better

Instead of saying *"Here are my projects"*, you're showing **what each project teaches**. A beginner can immediately see:

* **What topic to learn first**
* **Which project to build to practice it**
* **What skills they'll gain**
* **How the topics connect into a complete frontend roadmap**

This approach is more educational, easier to follow, and makes your portfolio or README look like a structured learning guide rather than just a collection of projects.
